An Introduction

Introduction Greetings, my name is Srivatsan Sudhir. I’m 16 years old and in 11th grade. I’m currently studying in Inventure Academy in Bangalore, India. I enjoy playing chess, watching films and tv shows, and reading. I currently have an FIDE rating of 1030 in chess and have also written multiple articles and short stories for my school newspaper. I also self-published a novel in 2020. I decided to start writing this blog because I quite enjoy writing, and wanted a platform to put out my thoughts on things that I don’t always have a chance to, while also furthering and honing my writing skills.
